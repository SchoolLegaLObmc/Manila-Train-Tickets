LRT-1

The National Railway

The LRT-1 is Manila’s premier elevated railway system, seamlessly connecting key districts and revolutionizing urban mobility with speed and efficiency. As the city’s first light rail transit line, it has become an essential lifeline, offering a reliable and convenient mode of transportation for millions. More than just a transit system, LRT-1 is a testament to progress, designed to elevate the everyday commute with comfort and ease.
